Taichung Veterans General Hospital


Web - Homepage Redesign

Time:August 2018
Client:Taichung Veterans General Hospital
User:General Public
Project Type:Web UI Design - Redesign
Role:UI Designer
ToolAdobe Illustrator, Sketch
Member:Wan-Ting Liao, Chien-I Chang, Min-Fang Hsu

Overview

The contents of the website of Taichung Veterans General Hospital (VGHTC) can be categorized into hospital enterprise information, hospital information, medical information, and hospital media; the main users of the website are general public. In order to improve the readability and usability of the website, which allows the viewers to quickly adapt to the website and obtain target messages after entering the homepage. Therefore, it is recommended that the content of the homepage be redistributed according to its function and separated by color blocks/areas to improve the legibility of the content.

Proposal

Original Web UI：

Proposal

1. Adjust colors and font: improve contrast of colors and use bold fonts for some titles or contents to improve clarity of reading.

2. The unity of visual elements and style: extend the homepage block to all pages. The icon's shape is more rounded to match overall vision and the visual of the information chart is unified.

3. Integration of information by different functions: recategorize the contents of the website, including different information such as hospital enterprise information, hospital information, medical information, hospital media…etc, and rearrange the order of different functions to improve the users’ understanding of the website and its usability.

4. Increase the use of photos: appropriate use of textured photos in media areas, such as carousel pictures, VGHTC Medical News, and patient feedbacks. It is helpful to increasing the user's impression of VGHTC and enhance its image.
