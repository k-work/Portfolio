Piano Room Booking

Role: UX Researcher, UI Designer
Tool: Adobe Illustrator, Sketch, InVision

Overview

Piano Room Booking is an app designed for the students of the Department of Music, NTHU. The app aims to shorten the time spent for booking piano practice rooms, which conventionally takes a long period of time. With this app, students could book piano practice rooms faster and more convenient.

Situation

There are two types of piano practice rooms in the Department of Music, NTHU: upright piano room and grand piano room. Students should book the rooms in advance through the Department office in a first come first serve fashion. There are two booking periods everyday: 8 am and 3 pm. The first round of reservation will be opened at 8 am to book available practice rooms during the 8 am~3 pm time slots. Another round of reservation will be available at 3 pm for students to book practice rooms during the 3~11 pm time slots. Each student could only fill up for one hour time slot per round.

Problem

The booking periods are not flexible for students. The time slots for the practice rooms will often be filled up within 15 minutes. In most cases they can not arrive to the office in time, constantly unable to reserve a practice room, or most of the time their desired time slots are not available.

Solution

Design an appoint system mobile app that could assist students in booking practice rooms via mobile device. This could improve the flexibility and efficiency of booking a practice room, lowering the time spent and the distant traveled for students, allowing them to focus on their practice and study.
